About Project

Students often experience stress because of assignments, internships, placements and examinations. This platform helps students monitor their daily well-being, understand burnout risks and improve their lifestyle through continuous self-monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Burnout?

Burnout is mental and physical exhaustion caused by prolonged stress.

How is burnout calculated?

The system analyzes stress, mood, sleep, study hours and motivation.
